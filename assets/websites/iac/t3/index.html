About Us

Jai Hind College (Empowered Autonomous) Presents

A safe harbour for young business ventures.

Founded in 2020, the Incubator & Accelerator Centre supports young business ventures navigating the challenges of their start-up stage.

At Jai Hind College, we believe in contributing proudly to the nation by creating a wholesome start-up ecosystem — mentoring and supporting budding entrepreneurs under the esteemed guidance of Principal Dr. Vijay Dabholkar, Academic Advisor Dr. Ashok Wadia and Academic Head Dr. Rakhi Sharma.
