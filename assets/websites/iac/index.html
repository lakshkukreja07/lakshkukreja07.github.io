About the centre

A safe harbour for the start‑up stage

The Jai Hind College (Empowered Autonomous) Incubator & Accelerator Centre exists for ventures that are still finding their footing — early, unproven, and worth backing anyway.

We believe in contributing to the nation by building a wholesome start‑up ecosystem: mentoring and supporting budding entrepreneurs under the guidance of our Principal, Academic Advisor and Academic Head.

"Every startup that comes through our doors gets a ring added to what they already know — mentorship, not instruction."
