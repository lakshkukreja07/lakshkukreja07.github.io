01 / ABOUT IAC

A PLACE FOR IDEAS
TO FIND DIRECTION.

Jai Hind College Incubator & Accelerator Centre is dedicated to nurturing student entrepreneurship through an ecosystem of startup support, mentoring, intellectual-property literacy, networking and practical exposure.

The aim is simple: help student founders move from early thinking to stronger ventures with access to the right people, spaces and opportunities.
