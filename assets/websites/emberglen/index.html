Our Story

Born from the Mist of the Highlands

Emberglen was built on a simple belief — that the ritual of a fine drink should never require a hangover. Deep in the Scottish Highlands, where ancient herbs grow wild and the air tastes of earth and rain, we found our ingredients.

Each bottle is hand-crafted using cold-drawn botanical extraction, a process that preserves the full complexity of every herb and flower. No shortcuts. No compromise.
