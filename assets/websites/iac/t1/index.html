The centre

A safe harbour for young business ventures.

The Jai Hind College (Empowered Autonomous) Incubator & Accelerator Centre is a safe harbour for young business ventures grappling about in their start-up stage.

Jai Hind College believes in contributing to the nation by creating a wholesome start-up ecosystem by mentoring and supporting budding entrepreneurs under the guidance of Principal Dr. Vijay Dabholkar, Academic Advisor Dr. Ashok Wadia and Academic Head Dr. Rakhi Sharma.

Fostering a culture of innovation — from an early idea to a stronger entrepreneurial journey.
