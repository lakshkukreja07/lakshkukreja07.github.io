About Vendora

Vendora is a B2B-focused vending machine company that provides smart, reliable, and scalable automated vending solutions for businesses across sectors. Our machines are designed to serve everyone—enhancing accessibility, efficiency, and convenience in workplaces, institutions, and commercial spaces.

By combining technology with operational simplicity, Vendora helps organizations streamline product distribution while delivering a seamless, modern experience to end users.

General FAQs

  • What is the warranty period for Vendora machines?
    All Vendora smart vending machines come with a standard 2-year comprehensive warranty covering parts and labor. Extended warranty options are available for enterprise clients.
  • What payment methods are supported?
    Our machines support UPI, QR codes, credit/debit cards, and app-less payments via WhatsApp/SMS. We also offer enterprise billing options for corporate clients.
